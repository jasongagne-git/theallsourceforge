The Governance Gap

Why isn't governance working?

3,533 cases across 49 countries show that more governance frameworks correlate with more incidents, not fewer. The problem isn't missing rules — it's that rules alone don't change behavior.

Governance Gap Research Published (SSRN)
Connected: SENTINEL experiments confirm this empirically — agents pass governance checks while drifting in practice. Signal briefs track these failures as they happen in defense programs.
AI Builders & Innovators

What's measurably breaking?

Multi-agent AI systems drift from their assigned behavior — and the monitoring meant to catch it may be masking it. 79 controlled experiments, 36,000+ agent messages, 112 findings. Open source.
